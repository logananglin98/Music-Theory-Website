Learn about Music Today!

The goal of this website is to give visitors a basic understanding of music theory. With the knowlege provided your sheet music reading skills will improve, you will be able to hear the components of a song and know their function, and perhaps even pick up an instrument and play with a basic understanding of what's going on!

Topics:

  • Music staff and components
  • Rhythm, note lengths, and rests
  • Scales, and how they are used
